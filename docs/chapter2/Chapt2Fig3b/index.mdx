### MATLAB - Chapt2Fig3b.m

```matlab title="Chapt2Fig3b.m"
%Chapt2Fig3b.m%200 THz electric field modulated by Gaussian envelope;clear;clf;tau0=14.14e-15;							%time constant (s)tau02=tau0^2;w0=2*pi*200.e12;							%200 THz optical frequency (rad s-1)npoints=500;								%number of points plottedwmax=2.e15;									%maximum frequency (rad s-1)wstep=wmax/npoints						%time step (s)w=[0:wstep:wmax];							%time [start:increment:stop]   y=(1/2)*exp((-(w-w0).^2)*tau02/2);  %divide spectral intensity by tau02figure(1);plot(w,y,'b');								%electric field intensity as function of frequencyaxis([0,wmax,0,0.5]);temp2=sprintf('Chapt2Fig3b, w0=%5.3e rad s-1, tau0=%5.3e s',w0,tau0);title(temp2);xlabel(['Frequency, w (rad s-1)']);ylabel(['Spectral intensity / tau0^2']);
```
